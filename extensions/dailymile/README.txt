  Dailymile Pytrainer extension
  ===============================
  This extension allows you to post your sport record directly 
  into your Dailymile activity stream.

  USAGE
  =====
  Click the `Post to Dailymile` button on the record view of an activity to
  post a summary of your workout on your Dailymile stream. The first time you
  use the feature you will be asked to log in to your Dailymile account and to
  allow Pytrainer to access your data.
